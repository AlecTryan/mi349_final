Meet Me

I am Alec Tryan, a full-time student at Michigan State University with a major in Games and Interactive Media with minors in both Game Design and Development and IT Management.

​

​Since I was in middle school, I have been recording and analyzing my reactions while playing games to better understand how they are designed and make players feel different emotions. I hope to use my interests in world history and psychology to contribute an interesting perspective in the field of game design.
